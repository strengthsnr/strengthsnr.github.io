About Me

I bring data and finance together to examine what drives performance, where value is created, and what the numbers reveal beneath the surface. Through quantitative and financial analysis, I turn complex information into a clearer view of performance, risk, opportunity, and future direction.

With experience in Excel, Power BI, SQL, data modelling, and PowerPoint, I translate complex business and financial data into structured analyses that clarify performance, trends, and underlying drivers. This portfolio brings together selected work across data analytics, financial analysis, and business intelligence, applied to a range of real-world business contexts.
